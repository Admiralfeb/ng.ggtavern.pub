How it works

All you need to do is ask the bartender to add a membership to your tab. You'll get the perks that day/night!

Your membership card is your photo ID. The small barcode on your ID acts as your membership number. We don't keep any other personally identifiable information.

You'll need to remind the bartender when you want to use your membership.
